About New Temple

Welcome to New Temple Collective, your exclusive retreat for styling and rejuvenation. Within these walls, it's not just about haircuts; it's about crafting an individual narrative for each client. New Temple Collective was born from a moment of transforming stress into art during a spontaneous haircut for a friend 3 years ago. In this intimate setting, you're not just a client; you're a part of a unique story. Every trim and style is meticulously tailored to reflect your style and bring forth a sense of relaxation. Experience the art of styling in solitude, where the focus is entirely on you. Welcome to the essence of New Temple Collective, where your distinct journey converges with the art of personal styling.
